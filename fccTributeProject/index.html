2011 Yaroslavl Lokomotiv Hockey Club Team Photo

In Memory Of Those Affected By The Lokomotiv Yaroslavl Air Disaster

On 7 September 2011, professional hockey team Yaroslavl Lokomotiv was scheduled to play a game in Belarus. As they began their journey to Belarus, their plane failed to gain altitude, ran off the runway & crashed just outside of the Tunoshna Airport (where they departed). Of the 45 on board, 43 died at the scene. Of the two rescued, only flight engineer Alexander Sizov survived.
